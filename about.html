Understand Your Heart Before You Begin

Cardiovascular disease is the leading cause of death worldwide. Many risk factors—including diabetes, high blood pressure, smoking, obesity, and high cholesterol—can silently damage the heart for years before symptoms appear.

🩸 Diabetes & Blood Sugar

Persistently high blood sugar can damage blood vessels and heart muscle, increasing the risk of cardiovascular disease.

❤️ Many Risk Factors Work Together

High blood pressure, smoking, unhealthy diet, lack of exercise, obesity, and family history all contribute to heart disease.

⚠️ Early Prevention Matters

Understanding your personal risk can help you make healthier lifestyle choices and know when to seek medical advice.
