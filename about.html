🫀 What is Diabetic Cardiomyopathy?

Diabetic cardiomyopathy (DCM) is a heart condition that can develop in people with diabetes, even without coronary artery disease or high blood pressure.

Why should you know about DCM?

  • 🩸 High blood glucose can gradually damage heart muscle.
  • ❤️ DCM may develop silently without obvious symptoms.
  • ⚠️ Early awareness can lead to earlier medical evaluation.
